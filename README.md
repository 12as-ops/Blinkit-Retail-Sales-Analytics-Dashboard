Blinkit Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard

# Project Overview

This project is a business-focused retail analytics solution built using Power BI for **Blinkit** retail sales data.

The main objective was to analyze:

* Sales performance
* Customer satisfaction
* Inventory distribution
* Outlet efficiency

The goal was to convert raw data into actionable business insights using KPIs and interactive dashboards.

This dashboard follows a structured analytical workflow:

* Business Requirement
* KPI Design
* Data Analysis
* Visualization
* Insights Generation

---

# Business Requirement

Conduct a comprehensive analysis of:

* Sales performance
* Customer satisfaction
* Inventory and product distribution

Identify opportunities for optimization using KPIs, trends, and visual storytelling.

---

# KPI Metrics

* Total Sales – Overall revenue generated
* Average Sales – Revenue per transaction
* Number of Items – Total products sold
* Average Rating – Customer satisfaction score

---

# Analytical Questions Solved

Sales by Fat Content

* Impact of fat content on revenue
* Comparison of ratings and item count

Sales by Item Type

* Best-performing product categories
* Product demand trends

Sales by Outlet

* Tier-wise comparison (Tier 1, Tier 2, Tier 3)
* Outlet size impact on revenue
* Establishment performance

Location Analysis

* Geographic revenue distribution

Outlet Type Analysis

* Grocery vs Supermarket performance comparison

---

# Key Results

* Total Sales: $776.32K
* Average Sales: $141
* Total Items Sold: 5,517
* Average Rating: 3.9
* Years Covered: 2012–2022

---

# Insights

* Tier 3 outlets generate higher revenue than Tier 1 and Tier 2
* Snack and household items dominate overall sales
* Low-fat products perform better in terms of demand
* Certain outlet types consistently achieve higher ratings
* Sales peaked around 2018
